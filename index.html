HIT YoUR TARGET WITH EEZY

Eezy deliver highly targeted, results oriented online marketing campaigns.

We use advanced Real-Time Bidding (RTB) technology and our deep knowledge to identify and purchase, in real-time, the display media that perfectly matches the profile of your target customer. As a client, you only pay for ad placements that fit your target market. And because of superior targeting, you also achieve higher conversion rates. This all translates into a higher ROI. In fact, we’re so confident in our ability to target the right customers that we frequently work on a performance basis.

That means you only pay us when you get sales. We run campaigns throughout Europe for top advertisers such as Sony, Allianz, Delta Lloyd, Halfords and Leen Bakker. Partnerships with ad exchanges such as AppNexus, Facebook Exchange, Microsoft Exchange and Google Exchange enable us to access top quality inventory. Thanks to partnerships with these and other major premium networks and publishers, we have a worldwide reach.
