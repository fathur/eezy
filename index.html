Precise targeting saves marketer €60.000!

The Chalenge

Imagine a marketer who wants to reach a very specific target group: young families with more than two children. Moreover, since the marketer’s budget is limited, they want to reach their target as efficiently as possible.

The Solution

In order to run an efficient campaign, Eezy helps the client create a detailed profile of the target group. Based on this profile, we discover that the target group is approximately one million consumers out of the total Dutch population of nearly 12 million internet users.

To fit within the client’s budget yet precisely reach this market segment, we configure the frequency cap for six “touches”; there will be six contacts with the prospect. We’re then able to reach the specific target group with 2 CPM and a budget of only €12,000.

The Results

Instead of searching for the target group among 12 million Internet users at 1 CPM and a cost of €72,000 – which is like searching for a needle in a haystack – the client is able to target exactly the right people. Moreover, instead of paying 7.2 cents per contact, the client only pays 1.2 cents per contact, thereby saving €60,000.

High conversion rate helps health insurer earn €52 for every €1 spent

The client

The client is a major Dutch health insurance company that has been in existence for more than 80 years and insures more than 800,000 people.

The challenge

The client wanted to reach a broad target group: all adults (i.e. those over the age of 18 years). Given the broadly defined target audience, efficiency and a high conversion rate were major concerns.

The solution

For maximum effect, the campaign needed to be carried out with intensity. Eezy ensured that over 30 million impressions were served in just over a month.

The results

Although the campaign appealed to a very broad market segment, Eezy was able to construct it so as to realize a post-click conversion rate of 7.12%. The high conversion rate contributed to an impressive ROI: for every €1 invested in the campaign the health insurer earned €52.

Contextual and semantic targeting boost campaign CTR

The challenge

A Dutch company that sells forklift accessories wanted to grow their business in the United States. They wanted both to become better known and to generate more sales.

The solution

We used contextual and semantic targeting in relevant industries to gradually optimize the campaign’s targeting.

The results

In the last week of the relatively short month-long campaign, we realized a Click-through Rate (CTR) of 0.319%, a large increase over the initial CTR of 0.034% during the first week of the campaign.

The campaign has helped this Dutch exporter to generate solid leads and achieve more success in the US market.

Psychographic targeting using Motivaction’s Mentality™ model yields high CTR

The challenge

A major health insurance company wanted to better target potential customers based on psychographic – rather than purely demographic – factors. These include factors such as lifestyle, personal views and values.

The solution

We set up a campaign modeled on the mindset of prospective customers in their database. Taking the two most relevant customer characteristics according to Motivaction’s Mentality™ model, we targeted two campaigns on the specifics of these two groups.

The results

The result of these two highly targeted campaigns was a Click-through Rate (CTR) of 0.215% on average over the two months of the campaigns and days when the CTR was as high as 0.375%.
